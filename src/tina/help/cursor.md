Places two frequency markers on the trace and reports the measured value at each, along with their difference.

## Delta Calculation

The delta between the two cursor readings is computed as:

$$\Delta = v_2 - v_1$$

Where $v_1$ is the value at Cursor 1 and $v_2$ is the value at Cursor 2.

For example, if Cursor 1 reads −10 dB and Cursor 2 reads −15 dB, the delta is −5 dB.

For background on subtraction, see [Wikipedia: Subtraction](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Subtraction).
